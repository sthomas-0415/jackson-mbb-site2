Welcome

This site collects and displays stats for college basketball teams in the Jackson, TN area. It helps fans find information easily and helps teams improve using AI-driven insights.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to make basketball stats easy to access and understand. We aim to use AI to provide insights like team strengths, weaknesses, and game predictions.

AI Features

  • Automatically updated stats and standings
  • Game and performance predictions
  • AI analysis of team strengths and weaknesses

About the Owner
